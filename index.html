F.A.Q.

So what exactly is Beeylo?

Beeylo is a new communication platform that brings all your conversations with your favorite brands into one clear, streamlined place. Built for the way we live today.

Does it cost anything to join?

Nope. Beeylo is and will always be free to use.

What happens after I join the waitlist?

You’ll be added to our exclusive waitlist. We’ll keep you updated on the launch, and you’ll be the first to get access. As an early access member, you get to help shape Beeylo, give feedback that matters, and secure your username before anyone else.

Can I use my current email with Beeylo?

Absolutely. Beeylo connects to your existing email address and turns scattered messages into one smart, organized ticket.

Can I use beeylo on desktop?

Beeylo is currently optimized for mobile, but we’re working on a full desktop version.

What happens to my data?

We only use your name and email to keep you informed about Beeylo. We will never share your information.
